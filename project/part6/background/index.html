Background

My Interest

I've been interested in aviation in general for a long time. My dad is a commercial pilot, so growing up I heard about and experienced a lot of aviation related events. I went to a lot of air shows with my dad and enjoyed them a lot, and over time my enjoyment of flying grew further and further. When I was 17 I flew an airplane for the first time and worked towards getting my private pilot's license. As my enjoyment grew I also began to wonder what the job market was like. Especially since my dad was in the field, I wanted to learn more about what it was like being a pilot for hire. This evolved to my curiosity of the extent to which major airline companies demanded more experienced pilots.

My Experiences

- 45 logged flight hours to date.

- 10 hours solo flight time.

- 5 hours IFR flight time.

- More than 50 logged landings of different aircraft.

- Novice level knowledge of C-172 flight abilities and general information.

- Over 6 hours cross country flight time.
