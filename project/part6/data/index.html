Analysis

The first reason as to why the aviation industry will likely experience growth within the future despite the current shortage is through the Air Force's efforts in increasing the amount of readily available teachers for new pilots. The pilot shortage will lead to a projected increase in the number of flight instructors due to the fall off in the number of experienced pilots and the increase in the amount of new people interested in flying. Because of this, the amount of pilots flying for the Air Force as of now might be below what is necessary, but the numbers will likely increase in the near future as the Air Force will likely hire more undergraduate pilot training instructors to compensate for the inability to retain experienced pilots. This should help remedy the issue of not being able to retain their best pilots by increasing the number of new pilots.
